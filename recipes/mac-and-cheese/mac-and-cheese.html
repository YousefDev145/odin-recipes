Mac and Cheese

Introduction

In this recipe, we're going to make a quick, easy, and tasty macaroni and cheese dish. It is a cheap alternative to the fancy, designer mac and cheese that often costs forty to fifty dollars. This delicious meal should not take more than 25 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) box elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the elbow macaroni in a pot of boiling water for about 8 minutes
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat
  3. Add flour, salt, and pepper and stir until smooth, about 5 minutes
  4. Pour in milk slowly, while stirring continuously. Continue to cook and stir until mixture is smooth and bubbling, about 5 minutes
  5. Add Cheddar cheese and stir until melted, 2 to 4 minutes
  6. Drain macaroni and fold into cheese sauce until coated
